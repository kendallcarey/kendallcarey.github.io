Looking back at Feedback

I felt at the beginning of Phase 0, feedback was incredibly important to me. I viewed it every week (a couple times a week, sometimes the same feedback over and over) to see how I could improve and if others felt like I was on the same level as them or at least keeping up. I was pretty insecure at the beginning. Like a few others, I came in with zero experience and was so nervous about being the worst in the class. But as I improved, I didn’t feel as anxious to look at the feedback. I still do check it because it is important as everyone can improve but not as religiously. I feel a bit more confident. I’ve noticed as we’ve all improved and learned to work together, its become harder and harder to actionable feedback. Kind feedback is easy because I like everyone that I’ve worked with. Specific feedback is a bit tougher especially if you want to keep it anonymous. When I’ve received specific feedback, I can almost always tell whom it was from. I will continue to give actionable feedback as often as I can and reading over the feedback I have received.

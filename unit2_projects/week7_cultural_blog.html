Looking back

The first week’s blog, I discussed my four of my values and the main one I wanted to talk about was courage. Last week, my blog was about stereotype threat and I talked about my fears as a female coder in training. I believe it takes courage to work past stereotype threat and do your best in a career field where many have low expectations for you or even expect failure. Its hard to overcome the negative thoughts in your head telling you that most people won’t expect you to make it in web dev. I keep reminding myself how much I want this and that success will be that much sweeter.

I was just talking to some male software engineers about Dev Bootcamp and what we were studying. One of the guys said that he had wished he had taken a different career path and the other guy shushed him saying, “Don’t scare away the only girl who wants to be a web developer!” I know the other girls in my cohort have probably heard similar jokes. In case any of the women in our cohort had any doubts and needed encouragement: Girls, we got this! And contrary to popular stereotype, we are not alone.
